About Mithas

A Family Recipe, Scaled With Care

Mithas began in a small Tamil Nadu kitchen with one belief: ice cream is only as good as the milk in it. Three decades on, that belief still runs every line in our ISO-certified unit.

Est. since the early days of Vellavedu Village
Our Story

From Vellavedu Village To Your Freezer

Every batch is built on fresh, hygienic dairy sourced close to home, then crafted in small runs so taste never gets sacrificed for speed. Our production unit can scale for festival demand without ever cutting a corner on quality.

Whether it's a stick kulfi melting on a summer evening or a family tub shared after dinner, the goal stays the same — milk that tastes like milk.
